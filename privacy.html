Privacy Policy

This page is used to inform website visitors regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use my Service.

Information Collection and Use

The app "Heritage Around the World" does not require you to provide it with certain personally identifiable information. The images taken with the QR-code scanner are retained on your device and are not collected by me in any way.

The app "Heritage Around the World" does not collect log data from your device.
